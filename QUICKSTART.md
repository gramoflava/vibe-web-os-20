## ğŸ› Troubleshooting

### Wallpaper Not Loading?
- Make sure the URL is a direct link to an image
- Check if the image supports CORS
- Try: `https://picsum.photos/1920/1080` (always works!)

### Windows Not Dragging?
- Click and drag from the titlebar (the top bar with the close/minimize/maximize buttons)
- Make sure you're not dragging from the content area

### Spotlight Not Opening?
- Use `Cmd+Space` (Mac) or `Ctrl+Space` (Windows/Linux)
- Click the ğŸ” icon in the menu bar

### Files Not Persisting?
- Make sure localStorage is enabled in your browser
- Check browser console (F12) for errors
- Try clearing cache and reloading
